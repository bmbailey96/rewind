Free/Ad-Supported Coverage (Watchmode)

TMDB's data (JustWatch) sometimes misses free ad-supported platforms like Tubi and Pluto. Get a free key at api.watchmode.com/requestApiKey (2,500 free requests/month, no card needed) to double-check Your Card against those when TMDB comes up empty. Optional, everything else works fine without it.

Sync Across Devices

Keeps Your Card, seen list, and skip list in sync everywhere you open this site, using a private GitHub Gist. Make a token at github.com/settings/tokens (classic token, only needs the "gist" scope), paste it below once per device. Connecting will pull whatever's already synced and apply it here, so do this on the device with your existing data first.
